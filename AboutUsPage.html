About Us

About Company

A Trusted & Leading Importer of First-Class Granites, Marbles, & Quartz Since 1984.

 

Who We Are?

 

We are the purveyors of quality marble & stone products from all over the world!

 

For over 35 years, we have been importing world-class natural stones from different parts of the world— India, Brazil, Italy, Spain, Portugal, France, Israel, Canada, Egypt, Peru, Mexico, and more. You can say we're a pioneer in the stone importing and distribution industry.

 

It couldn't be possible without our team members who traveled around the globe to source and hand-select the materials people love to have when they dream of putting stones in their homes, hotels, and business parks. We are always innovating and strive to use cutting-edge design and technology to help our customers turn their dream projects into reality.

Our Mission

Tapping into the unexplored market to deliver premium-quality natural stones and make a difference in people's lives through a timely supply of natural stone products in the USA.

Our Vision

We envision becoming the #1 choice for people when they idealize the design and look of their residential and commercial places with natural and engineered stones.

Our Approach

We endeavor each day to pay attention to detail & ensure our natural stone products are of premium quality & meet precision requirements-that is what customers demand.
