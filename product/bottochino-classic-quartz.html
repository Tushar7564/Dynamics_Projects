Why Choose Botticino Classic Quartz?

If buyers want the look of Botticino marble but don’t want to deal with its drawbacks, go with Botticino quartz. There are many reasons to pick this material apart from its beauty. Let’s look at some of the reasons to choose it below.

  • Durable: Botticino marble is beautiful, but Botticino quartz is beautiful and durable. It does not have any drawbacks that we see in marble surfaces. We guarantee that it will last you for decades.
  • Resistant: It is resistant to damage from scratches, stains, and chippings. It is excellent for use in high-traffic areas. Use it in the kitchen or bathroom and you’re good to go.
  • Non-Porous: It has a non-porous surface, it is non-absorbent. It makes the surface anti-bacterial and hygienic. It also makes every day cleaning an easy task.
  • Maintenance Free: Maintaining it is not a hassle when it comes to quartz. All it needs is water, a mild surface cleaner and a soft cloth. Besides, it does not need any sealing treatments.
  • Practical: White Calcutta quartz is a versatile and practical construction material. It has myriad applications in commercial to residential projects.
  • Affordable: Compared to other materials available on the market, it is affordable. Its price is worth it for the premium features and benefits it offers.
