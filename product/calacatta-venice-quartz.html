Reasons to Choose Calacatta Venice Quartz

There are many reasons to choose Calacatta White Quartz. Besides aesthetic value, it also has other functionalities and benefits to offer. That’s why designers, homeowners, fabricators, and architects value it alike. It’s also suitable for multiple projects including commercial and residential. Some of its benefits are its durability, easy maintenance, non-porosity, versatility, and more. Let’s look at the riveting reasons to choose Calacatta Venice quartz countertops below.

  • Durable: Venetian White quartz is a durable material option. It’s long-lasting compared to its inspiration – Calacatta Venetian marble & granite.
  • Easy to Maintain: It’s easy to clean and maintain the quartz slabs. As it is a manmade stone, it does not need annual sealing like natural ones do.
  • High Resistance: It’s resistant to heat, scratches, stains, and other damages. One does not have to worry about sudden damage due to the slab’s durability.
  • Non-Porous: Since it’s an engineered stone, it doesn’t have pores on the surface. It means it doesn’t absorb any liquid or moisture. The slab’s non-porous nature also makes it an anti-bacterial surface.
  • Versatile: It’s available in a wide range of colors, patterns, and finishes. Besides, it is suitable for both interior and exterior applications.
  • Affordable: It’s cost-efficient compared to Calacatta marble. Buyers can get Calcatta Venice at wholesale prices at Stone Depot.
