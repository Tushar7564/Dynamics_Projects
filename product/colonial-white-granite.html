Colonial White Granite is a stunning granite variant! It displays a blend of white and grey background with black and dark rose speckles. It’s well-known for its indoor functionality at chilly temperatures. If homeowners, designers, or architects need to modernize spaces, choose granite colonial white. Top Benefits of Colonial White Granite

There’s no doubt that the colonial white granite slab is magnificent. It has everything one would want in granite countertops colonial white. So, let’s look at some of the reasons you should choose colonial white over its counterparts.

  • Durable: Colonial white granite countertops are durable. They can endure most everyday impacts. But it’s advisable to not place hot utensils or chop vegetables directly on the slab.
  • Resistant: Their durability makes them resistant towards heat, scratches, stains, and chips. Keeping some small points in mind like using chopping boards can take your countertop a long way.
  • Hygienic: White granite countertops are easy to clean every day. So, there are low chances of bacterial growth.
  • Low Maintenance: They don’t need heavy and regular maintenance. Sealing them once or twice a year is enough.
  • Brightens Space: They can brighten any space they’re added to. Colonial white granite with white cabinets in the kitchen looks stunning.

Common Applications of Colonial White Granite

White granite countertops look excellent in multiple applications. Install them in interior or exterior projects and they’ll perform incredibly.

  • Wall cladding
  • Countertops
  • Floorings
  • Fountains
  • Staircases
  • Wall capping
  • Windowsills
  • Swimming pools
