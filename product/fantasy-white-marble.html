Maintenance of Fantasy White Marble

The mere installation of fantasy white marble countertops is not it. Maintaining your countertops is also important. Maintaining the black and white marble slab is not difficult. You must follow some simple steps for its longevity as follows:

  • Use a soft cloth and pH-neutral cleanser for cleaning.
  • Wipe any spills immediately to prevent stains.
  • Use coasters under mugs and glasses.
  • Place mats or trivets underneath hot dishes and kitchenware.
  • To avoid scratches, don’t place heavy objects directly on the marble.
  • For oil-based stains, mix baking soda and acetone.
  • Avoid using acidic or alkaline cleaners, such as vinegar or lemon juice.
  • To avoid cutting directly on the marble, use cutting boards instead.
  • Apply a high-quality marble sealant annually.
  • To keep the sheen on your marble, use marble polish on a regular basis.
  • Avoid excessive polishing, which might wear down the sealant.

Following these suggestions will help you preserve the beauty and durability of Fantasy White Marble surfaces.
