Fantasy Brown Leathered Marble?

There has always been confusion about whether fantasy is brown marble or granite. The color and white veins make it look like granite but on the other hand, the hardness aligns it more with marble. Also, its composition consists of calcium carbonate which classifies it as a marble.

Brown Leathered Marble is a natural stone featuring veins and patterns in beige, grey, and white colors on a brown surface. Brown fantasy granite’s durability and versatility make it a preferred choice in most construction projects.

It is also known by other names such as Aurora Fantasy Marble, fantasy brown India Marble, India fantasy brown marble, Glacier Sands Marble, and Cadbury Marble.  If you are looking for fantasy brown leathered granite, then contact Stone Depot.

Why choose Fantasy Brown Leathered Marble?

Exceptional Quality: It is a high-quality material suitable for making heavy furniture and other items since it is scratch-free.

Esthetic Appearance: Fantasy Brown Leather marble has an excellent blend of colors and textures and is an ideal natural stone that enhances the overall appearance of your house.

Heat Resistance: For kitchen and outdoor areas, you need a stone that can sustain the variations in the temperature. This marble is a perfect choice.

Durability: Its hardness ranges from 6 to 7 on the Mohs hardness scale, making it suitable for casual use.

Maintenance and Care: Fantasy Brown Leather marble is easy to clean and maintain. You can easily wipe out its lusterless surface with no threat of scratching.

Economical: Investing in this natural stone won’t go in vain since you get strength, beauty, and elegance – that’s all needed to remodel your home.
