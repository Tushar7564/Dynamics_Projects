Buy Calacatta Quartz at the Best Prices!

Calacatta Quartz countertops offer the best of both worlds – aesthetics and durability. The countertop replicates the natural appearance of Calacatta marble. The slabs exhibit a clean and bright white background with dramatic veining. The colors of veining can range from grey to gold. They have excellent features such as durability, resistance, consistent pattern, low porosity, and low maintenance. And getting all these attributes at a lower price point is a bonus.

Why Choose Calacatta Quartz?

The excellent features of Calacatta quartz countertops make it ideal for various applications. There’s no doubt about that. Yet, let’s look at some reasons to choose quartz Calacatta in detail. These attributes explain their popularity amongst designers, fabricators, and contractors.  

  • Durability: All quartz is durable and so are Calacatta white quartz countertops . They are manmade material that fills in every gap present in natural stones like granite. Minimal maintenance is enough for them to last for decades.
  • Low maintenance: Unlike granite or marble, quartz slabs do not need regular maintenance. They also don’t need annual sealing or waxing treatments. The smooth surface allows easy cleaning with water, a mild cleaner and a soft cloth.
  • Low porosity: Quartz is a non-porous material. It reduces the possibility of absorbing moisture, water, or any other liquid. The feature extends its longevity while also making it an anti-bacterial surface. No moisture retention leads to no bacteria breeding.
  • High resistance: The slabs are also resistant to heat, stains, and scratches. They’re tougher than natural stone countertops. It makes them an excellent material choice for busy areas like kitchens or bathrooms.
  • Consistent Pattern: It’s easier to find Calacatta ultra quartz in consistent patterns. It makes designing and creating cohesive spaces much more effortless. Finding granite or marble slabs with consistent patterns can be challenging.
  • Cost-effective: Buying quartz might seem expensive in the beginning. But it’s one of the most cost-effective material options. It’s because it offers value in the long run without any need for repairs or replacements.

Common Applications of Calacatta Quartz

Calacatta Nuvo quartz slabs are suitable for various applications. Their durable, non-porous, low maintenance and resistant nature are what makes them useful. No matter if it’s a household or commercial project segment, they are fit for every setting. Let’s look at some of the most common areas of Calacatta botanica quartz countertops below.

  • Kitchen Countertops: The first most common application area is the kitchen. They work excellently as kitchen countertops. They’re durable, non-porous and damage-resistant, which is everything a worktop should be. They can also work great as backsplashes.
  • Bathrooms: The next space is the bathroom. The quartz slabs are ideal for flooring, walling, and countertops as well. Remember to ensure buying a slip-resistant polish material for flooring purposes.
  • Wall Cladding: Homeowners can use calacatta river quartz to create beautiful wall accents. They are suitable for both residential and commercial spaces. Install them in the living or reception area, they’ll help bring out the best ambience there.
  • Fireplaces: They have high resistance against heat. So, they are an ideal material to use at the facing of fireplaces. Fireplaces can be a focal point in space and quartz can add further to their uniqueness.
  • Windowsills: Another common application area is the windowsills. Homeowners can use them to beautify the windows and add uniqueness to a simple window. It also makes cleaning the sills easier than wooden windowsills.
  • Staircases: Quartz Calacatta countertops can work well as staircases also. Installing them can make the stairs to the entryway look appealing and aesthetic. But buyers must remember to choose an anti-slip quartz finish when using them for stairs.
  • Tabletops: If homeowners are searching for tabletop materials, they can opt for quartz. They are suitable for writing tables, coffee table applications and more.
  • Sinks: Finally, fabricators can also use them to make sinks. They are a beautiful material that can make sinks an attractive bathroom feature.
