  • Description

Looking for high-quality and practical countertop material? Calacatta Laza Quartz is the way to go. It can elevate the ambience of any interior or exterior space. The quartz slab features a white background with a blend of soft and bold veining all over the surface.

The veining colors consist of brown and gold tints, giving a luxurious and refined look. Calacatta Laza Gold is durable, resistant, and never requires sealing. It is an excellent solution for both residential and commercial projects. Contact Stone Depot for Calacatta quartz slabs at the best prices.

Why Choose Calacatta Laza Gold Quartz?

There are not one but many reasons to select Calacatta Laza Gold Quartz. It’s always on trend and designers, architects, fabricators, and homeowners adore it alike. It offers buyers value for their money as it has everything that an ideal material must have. It’s beautiful, strong, long-lasting, resistant, easy to maintain and much more. Let’s look at the compelling reasons to choose the new calacatta laza gold below.

  • Aesthetic: Calacatta Laza Quartz has a great visual appeal. It adds luxury, refinement, and class to any space. The fine traces of brown and gold make it a breathtaking material. Still, the quartz is subtle enough to merge with any kind of style and design.
  • Durable: It is a durable material compared to other available options. It is an engineered stone made using different components. It consists of natural ground quartz, resins, and pigments. It will last longer than natural stones such as granite or marble.
  • Low Maintenance: It does not need sealing treatments like natural stones. The quartz Calcutta slabs need mere daily cleaning to keep up their beauty and shine. For cleaning, water, a mild surface cleaner and a soft cloth are enough. Wipe the countertop clean and you’re good to go.
  • Non-Porous: It is a manmade slab, so there are no porous on the surface. It means it does not absorb any liquid or moisture. The surface’s non-porosity makes cleaning effortless and makes it anti-bacterial. Thus, it’s also a hygienic material compared to other options.
  • Resistant: Quartz gold laza countertops have high resistance against external factors. They are resistant to stains, scratches, chips, and other damage. One does not have to worry about frequent repairs or replacements with gold laza quartz.
  • Practical: All the above-mentioned points make it a practical and versatile option. It has many uses, and it can cover both commercial and residential applications.
