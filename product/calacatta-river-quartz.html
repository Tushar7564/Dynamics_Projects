Why Choose Calacatta River Quartz?

There are many reasons to go for river quartz besides its appearance. No doubt that its striking veining pattern and soft colors give a luxurious feel. But there’s more, it offers great functionality, durability, versatility, and variety. Homeowners, designers, and fabricators can also put it to different uses. Let’s look at some solid reasons to choose river countertops below.

  • Durable: Calacatta grey river quartz, also known as river white quartz, is a manmade stone. Its build ensures that it’s long-lasting for years to come. When it comes to durable materials for countertops, quartz tops the list.
  • Easy to Maintain: It offers ease of maintenance. There’s no need to seal the surface to keep up its beauty and shine. All one needs to do is proper everyday cleaning.
  • Non-Porous: The surface of Calacatta riviera quartz is non-porous. It doesn’t absorb any liquid or moisture, so it becomes easier to clean. Also, the feature makes it anti-bacterial too.
  • Stain Resistant: It is more resistant to stains compared to natural stones like granite.
  • Versatile: The areas of application for the quartz river are many. It is fit for both indoor and outdoor use.
  • Variety: It is available in a wide variety of colors, patterns and finishes since it’s an engineered material.
