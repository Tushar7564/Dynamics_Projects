Panda White Marble

Panda White Marble is an excellent and unique natural stone material choice. It’s popular among homeowners, designers, and fabricators worldwide. The natural stone displays black and white monochromatic veins all over the surface. Its timeless beauty and polish give any project a statement look. Benefits of Using Panda White Marble

The advantages of using white panda marble countertops are many. Here are some compelling reasons to use them in different applications.

  • Long-lasting: Panda white marble slabs are durable and strong. It’s one of the stone’s main benefits. Its long-lasting nature is what makes it worth it in the long run.
  • Low Maintenance: Panda marble countertops are easy to maintain. All it needs is water, mild surface cleaner and a cloth to wipe the surface clean.
  • Affordable: The material may seem expensive initially. However, it’s very cost-effective when we look at the benefits it offers.
  • Eco-friendly: Panda marble India is a 100% natural stone. So, the process isn’t emitting any harmful fumes into the environment.
  • Many Finishes: It comes in a wide range of finishes from Bush Hammered, Leather, Antique, Polished, Lepatora, Honed to Brushed.

Top Applications of Panda White Marble

The applications of Panda white marble slabs are not limited to kitchens. Besides kitchen countertops, there are other uses of it too which we have mentioned below.

  • Kitchen Countertops
  • Pool liner
  • Interior and exterior walls
  • Furniture
  • House fronts
  • Shelves
  • Backsplashes
  • Columns
