Blue Dunes Granite

Color Dark Brown
Origin India
Slab Size Size:127×64
Applications Countertops, kitchen islands, accent walls, and floor tiles
Finish Leathered, Polished, honed
Thickness: 3cm

  • Description

Granite has been a popular material choice for a long time. And the new addition of granite is gaining a stellar reputation – Blue Dunes Granite. The granite hails from Rajasthan, India. It exhibits varying tones of black, grey, and cream with blue tones peeking through. It has an amazing dune-like pattern which gives the stone its name. If blue dunes granite countertops catch your eye, contact Stone Depot today.

Benefits of Using Blue Dunes Granite

Besides being a beauty, a blue dunes granite slab has many installation benefits. Its earthy tone, smoothness, durability, and versatility are attributes that make it so suitable. At Stone Depot, we offer custom-size orders for blue dune granite as per buyers’ preference. Below are some benefits of installing it in any space.

  • Long-lasting: Blue dunes leathered granite is a durable material choice. With minimal maintenance, it will last you a lifetime.
  • High Resistance: It has a high resistance against heat, scratches, stains, and impacts. The benefit makes it ideal for installation in high-footfall areas such as kitchens and baths.
  • Easy Maintenance: The blue dune granite countertop is easy to maintain. All it needs is annual sealing and a mild surface cleaner and water for daily cleaning.
  • Aesthetic Appeal: The natural stone offers great aesthetic value. It adds luxury and elegance to any project, no matter if it is commercial or home.
  • Versatile: Its neutral color makes it a versatile option as it blends seamlessly. Use it for cabinets, backsplashes and more, it looks stunning in every setting.
  • Finishes: It comes in multiple finish options including blue dunes leathered granite, polished, honed, brushed, antique and more.

Top Applications of Blue Dunes Granite

Blue Dunes Granite Texas has many applications. It is suitable for both internal and external installation. Here are some of the most common uses of blue dunes granite stone countertops below.

  • Kitchen Countertops
  • Backsplashes
  • Vanity Tops
  • Reception Areas
  • Pathways
  • Patios
  • Cabinetry
  • Outdoor Kitchen Floorings
