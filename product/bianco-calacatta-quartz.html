Buy Bianco Calacatta Quartz at the Best Prices

The market is switching to quartz for home and commercial applications. It’s because quartz options such as Bianco Quartz offer both beauty and longevity. With a shiny white background and unique grey veins, it gives an elegant and luxurious look. It suits all kinds of aesthetics, be it classic or contemporary. Choose Calacatta Bianco quartz for a beautiful, durable, resistant, and low maintenance stone.

Why Choose Bianco Calacatta Quartz?

There are many compelling reasons to choose quartz over natural stone countertops. It comes in different sizes making it suitable for tiles, floors, and other such uses. It mimics the stunning appearance of marble while offering more durability. Besides, it comes with different finishes that you can select as per the application. Let’s look at the top benefits of the bianco carrara quartz countertop below.

  • Durable: Bianco aspen quartz is durable and strong. The slabs last longer than natural stone countertops including granite and marble.
  • Easy to Maintain: The material’s low maintenance. Unlike granite or marble, it doesn’t need any surface treatment like waxing or sealing.
  • Easy to Clean: It’s also easy to maintain cleanliness. All it needs is water, a mild surface cleaner and a cloth to wipe the surface off.
  • Non-porous: It’s a non-porous material, meaning it doesn’t absorb any liquid or moisture. It further contributes to the stone’s longevity and makes it hygienic.
  • High Resistance: Bianco drift quartz has high resistance to heat, scratch, and staining. Yet, avoid direct contact between the surface and hot utensils.
  • Consistent: Quartz material such as Bianco dolomite quartz has consistent patterns. It’s easy to create a cohesive space using it, unlike granite or marble.
