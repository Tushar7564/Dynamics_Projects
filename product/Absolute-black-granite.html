Absolute Black Granite

  • Description

Absolute Black Granite is one of the most popular Indian-origin granite stones. It features a solid and consistent black color and texture. This stone is excellent for projects requiring aesthetics, durability, and high resistance. With its many benefits, practicality and uses, it offers lasting use and value for many years. It is best for both interior and exterior applications.

Types of Absolute Black Granite

Absolute black premium granite tile can be categorized based on different regions. It’s also known as Jet Black, Telephone Black, Nero Absolute, or absolute black Zimbabwe granite. Here are some types of absolute black granite based on region.

  • Warangal Black
  • Kothalwadi Black/Mysore Black
  • Khammam Black
  • Gumbali Black
  • Talwadi Black
  • Ilgur Black
  • Kanakpura Black
  • Panchpalli Black

How to Choose Best Absolute Black Granite?

When choosing the best Black Absolute Granite, there are a few factors to consider. These factors help buyers select high-quality granites from several options available. Let’s look at the significant pointers below.

  • Source: Granite absolute black is quarried from various locations worldwide. So, the quality can vary a lot depending on the source. It’s essential to contact a trusted stone supplier who can offer high-quality stones. Ensure the granite stones meet your specifications.
  • Grain Type: There are 3 types of grain sizes available in Black Absolute Granite – fine, medium, and big grain. The grain size determines the darkness of the stone. In the case of this granite, the finer the grain size the better. Choose a fine or medium-grain granite, both exhibit a dark color and excellent finish.
  • Slab Size: Selecting the right slab size is also important. The size of the granite slab depends on the project’s size. It’s crucial to make an accurate measurement of your needs. After which select a slab size that best suits your demands without causing any wastage.
  • Polish: This black granite variety comes in different surface finishes. It’s available in absolute black honed granite, polished, leather and more. Each finish offers a different appearance and feel to a space. So, ensure your selection matches your design and style preferences.
  • Scratches: Even small scratches on a dark-colored material are prominent. They can result from the mishandling of slabs. Make sure to spend a good amount of time inspecting the material before buying. In the end, good quality stones ensure well-done projects, residential or commercial.

Benefits of Absolute Black Granite

Absolute black granite slab offers innumerable benefits. These benefits are the reason why it’s popular among homeowners, builders, and designers. Apart from looks, other features make it a top choice in the industry. Let’s look at what makes granite absolute black, a unique natural stone option below.

Where They Come from (Which Country Produce Best Black Granite)

India is home to rich natural stone sources, and it produces the finest quality black granite. It is well-known for black granite quarries, and absolute black is no exception. The granite’s major mining location is Arpanapally Village, Warangal District in Telangana, India. Besides, South Africa, China, Uruguay, and Canada are also producers of this granite.

How to Clean Absolute Black Granite?

Absolute black is a durable granite requiring the least upkeep. Yet, it’s better to clean and maintain it regularly to keep it looking the best. One can clean this granite surface using different methods. But we will be sharing the simplest methods for cleaning and maintenance.

  • Daily Cleaning: Everyday cleaning of the granite surface requires a simple wipe. Mop the surface using water, a mild detergent, and a cloth, and it’s good to go. But, if there’s a stain on the granite, remove it using an appropriate stain remover only. Avoid using any harsh chemical cleaners on the surface at all costs.
  • Annual Sealing: Granite is not a completely non-porous surface. That’s why it’s essential to seal it on an annual basis. The sealing treatment enhances the surface’s resistance against scratches and tough stains. It also makes cleaning easier and prevents damage from daily use and exposure to heat, water, or oil.

FAQ:

1. Where is absolute black granite quarried?

Absolute black is quarried mostly in the Warangal District, Telangana. It is in southern India. Besides, it is also quarried in South Africa, China, Uruguay, and Canada.

2. How do I care for absolute black granite?

Taking care of granite absolute black is easy. All it needs is daily cleaning and annual surface sealing to keep up its visual appeal. Wipe the surface off using a mild surface cleaner, cloth, and water. Call a professional to seal your granite surfaces.

3. Can absolute black granite be used outdoors?

Yes, it is a tough and durable granite stone that is ideal for outdoor applications. Some common outdoor applications are outdoor flooring, swimming pool areas, exterior wall coverings, architectural features, and more.

4. How does absolute black granite compare to other granite colors?

Black granite performs well compared to other granite colors. The surface is less likely to fade as it’s both dense and dark in color. Also, it is very hard to wear, so it does not scratch, stain, or chip easily.
