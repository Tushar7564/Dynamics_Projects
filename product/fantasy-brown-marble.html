If you’re searching for a premium quality marble, consider Fantasy Brown Marble. It’s a high-quality Indian marble available at cost-effective prices. The marble slab exhibits chocolaty brown and off-white wave formation throughout the surface. Its aesthetic, durability, resistance, and versatility make it ideal for various applications. One can find brown fantasy marble in many finishes too such as polished and unpolished.

What is Fantasy Brown Marble? 

Fantasy Brown is not granite and definitely not quartzite. It’s neither hard like granite nor quartzite. It is a marble, but there’s a catch. It is harder than regular marble. That’s why, it’s also known as a “hard marble”, or “soft quartzite.” The reason behind this is the dolomitization process fantasy marble brown undergoes while formation. Some people also call it “dolomite”, or “dolomite marble.”  

Fantasy Brown Marble Comparison  

  Here’s a comparison of Indian fantasy brown with other material options.  

  • Granite vs Fantasy Brown : Granite is an igneous rock while fantasy brown is a metamorphic rock. Thus, they are not similar. Granite countertops are scratch and etch-resistant. Whereas fantasy brown countertops are susceptible to scratches and etching if not well-maintained.  
  • Quartzite vs Fantasy Brown : Quartzite and fantasy brown are both metamorphic rocks. But brown fantasy marble consists of calcite components which makes it brittle. Whereas quartzite does not have any calcite component. That’s why it is harder and scratch and etch-resistant. Brown marble fantasy is softer compared to quartzite. Even though both are a limestone kind transformed over time with heat and pressure.  
  • Quartz vs Fantasy Brown : Quartz is a manmade stone whereas brown fantasy is a natural stone. Both of their compositions are poles apart.  
  • Marble vs Fantasy Brown : Fantasy brown is marble but with a twist. It is harder and more resistant to damage compared to regular marble slabs. Marble scores between 3 to 5 on the Mohs scale while granite falls between 6 to 7 out of 10. Brown fantasy marble falls somewhere between the two.  

Why Choose Fantasy Brown Marble?

There are many compelling reasons to choose fantasy brown marble countertops. It has carved out a place for itself as an excellent option to use in projects. Marble might not be as hard as granite, but it’s hard enough for both interior and exterior spaces. Let’s look at some benefits of using the fantasy brown marble slab below.

  • Classy Look: Brown marble countertops give any space a warm and dramatic look. Whether it’s a commercial or residential project, expect a positive ambience after installation.
  • Durable: In general, marble is softer than granite. But the geological formation of fantasy wave marble adds to its uniqueness. It’s hard marble, meaning it’s tougher than its marble counterparts.
  • High Resistance: It’s resistant to heat, scratches, stains, and impact. But remember that even though the stone’s strong, it’s not indestructible. So, it’s better to always take precautionary measures to avoid damage.
  • Minimal Maintenance: Surface sealing of marble is a must to ensure its longevity. An annual sealing should be enough. For everyday cleaning, a mild surface cleaner and warm water is the best option.
  • Easy Installation: The fantasy brown countertop is not as heavy as compared to granite. So, the installation procedure is easy and quick for professionals.
  • Versatile: It’s a versatile material option with many applications. Its classy color can enhance the appearance of almost any space.

Various Applications of Fantasy Brown Marble

 

 

Every benefit discussed above makes fantasy brown a suitable option for many uses. It doesn’t scratch or etch easily like traditional marble. As per experts, it is best to go with honed or leathered finished marble. These finishes help avoid scratches and etching to a great extent . From kitchen countertops to shower walls, here are its most common applications.  

  • Kitchen Countertops
  • Bathroom Vanity Tops
  • Bar Tops
  • Fireplace Surrounds
  • Tub Surrounds
  • Shower Walls

How to Maintain Fantasy Brown Marble?  

Care and maintenance of brown fantasy marble is easy. A sealing treatment is enough to protect it from most damage. But you’d like to reseal it every year for the best results. For everyday cleaning, some water, a soft cloth, and mild surface cleaner will get the job done. If you want to be extra careful and want to maintain its shine and luster, re-polishing it is a good option.

Frequently Asked Questions

 

1. What is Fantasy Brown Marble?

It is a quarried Indian-origin natural stone marble available in stunning neutral colors, including white, grey, beige, green and earthy brown.

2. What is the Origin of Fantasy Marble?

The Fantasy Marble Slab is an Indian-origin natural stone from the northern region of India, Rajasthan.

3. What is the Life Expectancy of Fantasy Brown Marble?

Fantasy Brown is a long-lasting and reliable marble choice. Proper resealing once a year means you do not have to worry about it getting scratched, etched or damaged.

4. What are the applications of Fantasy Brown Slab?

It is excellent for kitchen countertops, tabletops, bathroom vanity tops, bathroom floorings, bar tops, accent walls, floor tiles, fireplace surrounds, tub surrounds, etc.

5. What are the Different Finishes in Fantasy Brown Slab/Marble?

The stunning marble is available in different finishes, from honed, polished, leathered, brushed, lepatora, and antique to bush hammered.

6. Is Fantasy Brown Granite, Quartzite or Marble?

Due to Fantasy Brown’s durability and hardness, it is often confused with granite or quartzite; however, it is a marble. It is harder than regular marble because of dolomitization.

7. Is Fantasy Brown scratch and etch-resistant?

The Brown Marble cannot be labelled completely scratch and etch-resistant; however, it does not scratch or etch as easily as a regular marble.

8. How to maintain Fantasy Brown Marble?

To maintain the Fantasy Brown Slab in top condition, reseal the marble once a year. Conduct a water test to find out if it’s time to reseal. If the marble absorbs the water, it’s time to reseal again.

9. Is Brown Fantasy Marble suitable for kitchen countertops?

It is an excellent choice for kitchen countertops considering the marble’s aesthetic appearance and price point.

10. Is Fantasy Brown Marble expensive?

Most natural stones that look as stunning as Brown marble cost much more than Fantasy Brown Slab, making the it a cost-effective option for construction projects.
