Reasons to Choose Calacatta Venatino Quartz

Venatino Quartz offers a perfect blend of charm, durability, resistance, versatility, and low maintenance. Besides, it has all the functional qualities that make it ideal for everyday living spaces. It is suitable for residential and commercial applications, be it indoors or outdoors. Here are some of the top reasons to pick quartz Venatino.

  • Durable: Quartz calcutta Venatino has a strong build. It’s an engineered stone made using – ground quartz, resins, and color pigments. Thus, it’s built to last for decades.
  • Low Maintenance: It is an easy-to-clean surface. It does not need any sealing or waxing treatment like natural stones. All one needs is water, a soft cloth and mild cleaner to wipe off any dirt and grime on the slab.
  • Resistant: It has high resistance to stains, scratches, chips, and other damage. So, it is excellent for areas with high footfall like the kitchen or bathroom.
  • Non-Porous: New Venatino grey quartz is non-porous. It means the surface is non-absorbent. It does not absorb any liquid or moisture that prevents the growth of bacteria and mold. It also makes daily cleaning a breeze.
  • Uniformity: There’s uniformity in the patterns of the slab. It helps homeowners, designers and architects create more cohesive spaces.
  • Versatile: Durability, easy maintenance, resistance, non-porosity, and uniformity make it a versatile material. It has different application areas and is suitable for various aesthetics. Whether one prefers a modern, traditional, or ornate look, it can fit all styles and designs.
  • Cost-effective: Calcatta Venatino grey quartz is affordable compared to the marble options. It offers the natural charm of marble without any of its drawbacks.
