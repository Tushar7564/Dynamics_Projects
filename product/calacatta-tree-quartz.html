If you’re looking for a neutral yet attractive material, we have the best option. Calacatta Tree Quartz is a stylish and luxurious surface. Why Choose Calacatta Tree Quartz?

Quartz tree is a one-of-a-kind material. Its unique appearance makes it a sought-after quartz material in the market. Besides, there are various other reasons to select Calacatta Tree Quartz. Here are some of the most compelling reasons to pick white Calcutta quartz .

  • Durable: The Tree Calacatta countertop is a strong and durable option. It’s made and built that can last you a lifetime. If maintained well, one doesn’t have to worry about repairs or replacements.
  • Maintenance Free: It requires the least maintenance to keep up its appearance. The engineered stone doesn’t need sealing or waxing treatments. A simple wipe using water, a mild surface cleaner and a soft cloth is enough.
  • High Resistance: It is high resistance towards stains, scratches, and chips. It cannot get damaged with everyday impact. This makes the Calcutta quartz excellent for high-traffic areas such as the Kitchen.
  • Non-porous: The material’s surface is non-porous. It does not absorb any liquid or moisture. So, the chances of bacterial growth are almost zero.
  • Functional: Calacatta Tree Quartz is functional and can be used in almost any project. It’s suitable for commercial and residential projects. Whether it’s an interior or exterior area.
  • Unique: The veining pattern makes it a unique material. They resemble the roots of the tree, which is unlike any other material in the market.
